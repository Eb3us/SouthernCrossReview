Oriphiel – The Angel of Power



by Annael

I am the Wind!
I am the Wind which descends from the Heavens...
My caresses fly towards the blessed trees of life;
my kisses touch the sharpness of their leaves,
and where hidden sadness is born,
where tears are shed,
my love will spread its wings
in consolation.

Let the clouds raise their faces,
let the blue colour of the sky grow like a summer fruit.
My love will fall like rain upon the breast of the Earth...
And then the Image of the One Who lives eternally
will suddenly appear
within the heart of every Man,
completely purified
and pious.

Annael is a professional artist working in oil painting, printmaking and illustration. For her, creativity is a means of expressing something of the Divine love, wisdom and truth present in Nature and in every human being. She creates her paintings using classical music, weaving in the works of composers from the Renaissance and Baroque to the Postmodern periods to form a unique synthesis of idea, music and visual art. Her monoprints are created using a highly original technique which she invented in the 1980's, enabling the production of bright, lucid colours in a way that has not been achievable since the 16th century. Numerous books and wine labels have been illustrated by her as well. Several of her works are in high-profile national and international collections. Besides her artistic work, Annael also writes poetry and children's stories. Annael can be contacted at annael@annael.com.
